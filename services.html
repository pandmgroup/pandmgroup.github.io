What is the advantage of using an insurance broker?

Cost – Contrary to popular consumer belief, studies have shown that brokers (whom are a free service) are cheaper that a direct insurer.

Why? Brokers are professionally trained to tailor the right policy for their clients, and not to uninsure. This avoids unnecessary claims, whilst maintaining the correct premium because of this, insurance providers will offer brokers special prices.

Having a broker will eliminate any communication when having to write a claim or take out a policy, as they mediate as the middleman to ensure there is no interruption in your business/personal time.

Peace of mind – as a broker will tailor and service the insurance cover and policies around you, they will take into consideration any pre existing conditions to ensure you if the worst where to happen, you would be covered and the case would not be dismissed because of a pre existing condition.
